Pubs in Bath

There are plenty of pubs in Bath, these are just a small selection of what's on offer!

The Bell Inn

 Previous Next

103 Walcot St, Bath BA1 5BW

The Bell is a large pub on Walcot Street with regular live music throughout the week. A very lively atmosphere both indoors and outside, they also serve Pizza on certain nights.

The Star

 Previous Next

23 Vineyards, Bath BA1 5NA

The Star is a very small pub near Nest. Although it's not very lively, it does do a wine and cheese night...

Lamb and Lion

 Previous Next

15 Lower Borough Walls, Bath BA1 1QU

Lamb and Lion is a hotspot for sports games such as Rugby and Football. It also has some nice decking and tables out back for summer time

The Boater

 Previous Next

9 Argyle St, Bath BA2 4BQ

The Boater is a Fuller's pub that has a lovely beer garden located right next to Pultney Weir & Bridge. They also do a BBQ outside on a sunday.

The King of Wessex

 Previous Next

5-10 James St W, Bath BA1 2BX

The King of Wessex is Bath's only Wetherspoons. Sometimes difficult to find a table but it's a cheap night out.

The Cork

 Previous Next

11-12 Westgate Buildings, Bath BA1 1EB

The Cork is a trendy pub located in the city centre about a 5min walk from Bath Spa train station. It has a pub quiz on a sunday aimed mainly towards students and does student discount on certain drinks.
