Sports in Bath

Bath nourishes the body as well as the mind. From rugby to horse riding, there is plenty of opportunities to play, cheer or simply watch your favourite sport!

Previous Next

Rugby

Whether you are a huge rugby fan or you are completely new to the subject, you will not regret a day at the Bath Rugby Stadium. The spirit of this sport has lived in the heart of Bath since 1865 and it surely marked the lives of many of its inhabitants. Grab a tasty bite during half time from the local

stands behind the stadium and enjoy live music while you get ready for the game. Games are a quick sell out so we reccomend getting your tickets early rather than later. Concessions and discounted tickets are available to students, children and adults 65+yrs.

Watersport

Exploring Bath from the water is an exciting way of getting to know the city from a new and completely different perspective. Hire a canal boat with family and friends for the day or for a short holiday to fully immerse in the Bath spirit.

Relax while floating around historic buildings on your own vessel or try different sports such us stand up paddleboarding or canoing. Beside being one of the best ways to spend your time in a hot day, it is also fun and perfect for big groups of friends.

Cycling and Walking

Bath is surrounded by rolling hills and tranquil sorrounding and with the countryside on the city's doorstep it would be a shame not to explore it! There are plenty of occasions to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature.

Take a short trip through the meadows of Bathwick Fields, cycle through the Bath Skyline walk to be rewarded with a stunning view of the city and go on the National Trust's most downloaded walk. Ancient woodland, abandoned limestone quarries and the Sham Castle are waiting for you!.
