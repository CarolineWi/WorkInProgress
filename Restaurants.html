Restaurants in Bath


Bath is home to many nice restaurants and has a lot of fancy tea rooms!


Bath has long been a tourist favourite due to its stunning Georgian architecture and thermal springs, with an abundance of things to see and do.

There is a huge range of restaurants, here is a snipet of the ones we prefer!
